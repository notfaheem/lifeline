# Life Line
> _Where did your time go ?_

Lifeline is a simple web project that allows you to visualise your day on a long bar cut into sections with titles resembling what did you do at that time.
It allows people to find where did their time go daily as the subheading says!
It also gives you an analysis of your day.

## Features
- Modern Minimal Design
- Easy to use
- Analysis of your day

## Safety
Your data is never leaving your device and we are not using it for any purposes.
